How do I find out what Time Off I am able to request?

Statutory leaves of absence vary between countries. Depending on your country of work you may be eligible to request time off for reasons such as vacation, sick leave, bereavement, jury service, military leave, care of dependents and marriage.

We have specific articles which you can refer to for information and country guidance on vacation and sick leave. If you require information about any other types of leave, please log a request with Global People (HR).

How much time can I take?

The amount of time you will be eligible to take will depend on the type of leave, your country of work, and your personal situation. If you are unsure, please contact Global People (HR).

How do I request Other Time Off?

Request Other Time Off (bereavement, jury, etc.), through Workday under the Request Time Off section and enter the reason for the request in the 'Comment' field.

How do I request a Leave of Absence?

To request a leave of absence (maternity, parental, etc.), please discuss the request with your manager and then submit a ticket here. In the comments section, you should add the reason for the leave request. 

Your request will then route to Global People for review before routing to your manager for approval.   

If you are taking leave and are unsure how this will impact your benefits,  please raise a request here and a member of the Global People team will support you.
