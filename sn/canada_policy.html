Paid parental leave - Canada

Overview - ServiceNow Paid Parental Leave 

The purpose of this article is to provide an overview of some of the key aspects of the ServiceNow Paid Parental Leave.

Birthing Parent: The parent who is going to give or has given birth to the child. 

  • 20 weeks (includes weekends and public holidays) of paid parental leave
  • To be taken for recovery from childbirth and to bond with a new child
  • Leave must be taken within one (1) year of child's birth
  • Leave runs concurrently with any other statutory leave

Review the: Canada Parental Leave Policy English/French or  FAQs for more information.

Non-Birthing Parent: The parent who has not given birth to the child.

  • 12 weeks (includes weekends and public holidays) of paid parental leave
  • To be taken for birth of a child, adoption, foster placement or receive a child through surrogacy
  • Leave must be taken within one (1) year of birth or placement of a child
  • Leave runs concurrently with any other statutory leave

Review the Canada Parental Leave Policy English/French or  FAQs for more information.

Pregnancy Leave & Pay

Paid Parental Leave will be inclusive of statutory pay, you will receive benefits available through the Employment Insurance scheme funded by the Canadian government, so that you receive 100% regular base pay when on ServiceNow Paid Parental. Subject to local requirements, in no event will you receive more weekly income than the amount of your regular weekly base pay.

Parental Leave (Baby Bonding) & Pay

Paid Parental Leave for a Non-Birthing Parent can be used for the birth, adoption, foster placement or surrogacy. The 12 weeks will run concurrently with any regulatory leave and you will receive benefits available through the Employment Insurance scheme funded by the Canadian government, so that you receive 100% regular base pay when on ServiceNow Paid Parental Leave. Subject to local requirements, in no event will you receive more weekly income than the amount of your regular weekly base pay.

You must coordinate the use of ServiceNow Paid Parental Leave with your manager.  In all cases, the entire ServiceNow Paid Parental Leave must be completed within one year after the birth, adoption, foster placement of a child or surrogacy.
