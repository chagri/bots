Important information (Macau)

  • This is applicable for premium payment and AIA Vitality membership fee only.
  • We can only accept cheques issued by policyholder or the insured.
  • For bank draft of US$60,000 or above (or MOP equivalent), please provide the bank receipt to confirm your identity.
  • To meet the regulatory requirements, if you make a cash transaction of MOP $120,000 or above (or US dollar equivalent) at OCBC Wing Hang Bank or Bank of China Macau Branch , you need to show your ID card and sign the deposit slip to allow the bank to transfer the information to us.
  • We do not issue a receipt when you pay at Bank. Please keep the deposit advice for your record.
  • Please make the payment in advance of the due date, as it takes 3 working days to process the payment.
