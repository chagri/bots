FPS

An individual Faster Payment System (“FPS”) QR Code will be pre-printed or shown on applicable Premium Notice, Grace Period Expiration Notice and AIA Vitality Renewal Membership Notification.

You can also pay short payment of new policy, renewal premium of General Insurance (only applicable for products with policy prefix of “C”), renewal fee of AIA Vitality membership or outstanding levy, please click here to pay via FPS in real time.

As every FPS QR Code is unique by linking up to individual policy number/ AIA Vitality Membership Number with its payable amount, you can settle payment by simply scanning the FPS QR Code by the scanner built-in in the FPS mobile application.

Important information

  • Premium Notice and Grace Period Expiration Notice: FPS QR Code will only be pre-printed / shown on Notices where the “total premium and levy amount” is displayed in Hong Kong Dollars or its equivalent, with payable amount up to HK$400,000.
  • AIA Vitality Renewal Membership Notification: Settling Renewal Membership Fee for AIA Vitality via FPS QR Code is only applicable to members in Hong Kong.
  • No receipts will be issued for payments made through FPS. Please keep the transaction number (with prefix of “FP”) provided by FPS as record.
  • Payment should be made well in advance of the premium due date as it takes 3 working days to process.
  • If payment is made from Non-eligible payor's account, we reserve the right to arrange refund to payor's account via original payment channel (as the case may be), charges incurred by Bank will be borne by the payor.
  • Payment or transfer limit is pre-set by bank / e-Wallet operator. Please contact relevant parties for details.
