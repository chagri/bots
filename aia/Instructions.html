Instructions

  1. If paying by cash, please visit our customer service centers, Hong Kong Post Office, 7-Eleven, Citibank or Bank of China branches in Hong Kong. Cash payment at the Customer Service Center is only applicable to the payment of premium, premium levy and AIA Vitality membership fee, not applicable to loan repayment or deposit into reserves.
  2. You can pay the premium and levy together by any of the following methods. If you have already paid the premium and now you only need to pay the premium levy (if applicable), please click here for the payment method. For details of the levy charged by the Insurance Authority, please click here .
  3. Please make all checks payable to "AIA (International) Limited".
  4. All premiums should be paid by the policy owner/insured.
  5. To pay the premium of each policy over USD 30,000 or equivalent in Hong Kong dollars by cashier's check, it is necessary to complete the " Premium Payment Statement - Cashier 's Check " or provide a valid customer receipt issued by the bank to prove that the cashier's check is issued by the policy owner/ The insured pays for the purchase; and a cashier's check for premiums over US$150,000 (applicable to new policies) or over US$300,000 (applicable to renewal policies) or equivalent Hong Kong dollar premiums must provide a valid customer receipt issued by the bank.
  6. For premium payment for investment-linked policies, we only accept Hong Kong dollars, local US dollar crossed checks or US dollar cashier's orders. Change of investment allocation / payment of additional investment premium / payment of flexible money / premium repayment during the payment suspension period must be submitted together with the "Investment-Linked / Universal Life Insurance Plan Service Application Form" or "Repayment of the "Suspension of Payment" period" Submit the premium application form together.
  7. You can use China Construction Bank (Asia) or BEA AIA credit card to pay premium, but not applicable to single premium payment, investment-linked policy, outstanding balance, premium payment reserve, additional investment premium and fleximoney. Other bank credit cards are only applicable to the monthly premium paid by autopay.
  8. If the premium is paid through the company's Hong Kong or Macau customer service center, the company will issue an official receipt to the policyholder.
  9. The premium payable should be paid before the due date or within the 31-day grace period. Before the grace period expires, the policy is still valid. If the premium is not paid within the grace period, the policy will lapse and lose value, except for the benefits provided by the existing cash value clause or premium suspension clause in the policy (if applicable).
  10. If payment is made by credit card / debit card and there is a refund later, AIA reserves the right to refund the refund directly to the credit card account of the credit card owner / bank account of the debit card owner (as the case may be) . For refunds involving currency conversion, banks or other third parties (such as merchant banks) will use the exchange rate determined by them or the relevant card associations (such as VISA, MasterCard, etc.), or the exchange rate applied by other third parties on the conversion date , plus any additional exchange rate adjustments by the bank (or credit card issuer, as the case may be) and any fees imposed by the card association (if applicable) to convert non-Hong Kong dollar transactions into Hong Kong dollars (and vice versa, subject to depending on the currency of the credit card or bank account). The exchange rate adjustment and costs will be borne by the payer.
  11. In addition to the exchange rate risk mentioned in the main product risk section of the product introduction, if the policy currency chosen by the customer is different from the currency in which the premium is paid, we will transfer the policy currency paid by the customer according to the prevailing market currency exchange rate (determined by us) Premiums are converted to the policy currency of their choice. Customers should pay attention to the exchange rate risk and decide whether to bear the risk.
  12. Premium conversion is applicable for payment in USD/HKD/MOP (applicable to policies issued in Macau only). In addition, we also accept RMB/CAD/AUD/GBP to pay premiums in the same policy currency. For the payment options available in different policy currencies, please check with your financial planner/insurance and financial advisor for details.
  13. If the payment amount is more than US$60,000 (or lower amount in some cases), the customer needs to imprint the credit card information on the credit card authorization form or provide a copy of the credit card to provide the cardholder's name and credit card number.
