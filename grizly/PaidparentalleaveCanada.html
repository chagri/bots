Paid parental leave - Canada

Overview - Grizly Paid Parental Leave 

Both birthing and non-birthing parents are entitled to parental leave. Birthing parent gets up to 16 weeks of paid parental leave while the non-birthing parent gets up to 12 weeks of parental leave.

Pregnancy Leave & Pay

Paid Parental Leave will be inclusive of statutory pay, you will receive benefits available through the Employment Insurance scheme funded by the Canadian government, so that you receive 100% regular base pay when on Grizly Paid Parental. Subject to local requirements, in no event will you receive more weekly income than the amount of your regular weekly base pay.

Parental Leave (Baby Bonding) & Pay

Paid Parental Leave for a Non-Birthing Parent can be used for the birth, adoption, foster placement or surrogacy. The 12 weeks will run concurrently with any regulatory leave and you will receive benefits available through the Employment Insurance scheme funded by the Canadian government, so that you receive 100% regular base pay when on Grizly Paid Parental Leave. Subject to local requirements, in no event will you receive more weekly income than the amount of your regular weekly base pay.

You must coordinate the use of Grizly Paid Parental Leave with your manager.  In all cases, the entire Grizly Paid Parental Leave must be completed within one year after the birth, adoption, foster placement of a child or surrogacy.
