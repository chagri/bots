    What is Ola Money?

    Ola Money is your gateway to a cashless world. Ola Money powered by Zipcash is a pre-paid instrument issued by Zipcash in co-branding with Ola, as per RBI guidelines.

    You can use Ola Money to pay for everything starting from your cab fares, mobile bills, DTH recharges, electricity & gas bills and for online shopping. Just recharge your Ola Money and enjoy a seamless experience!

    Some of our premium partners include BookMyShow, eBay, Dominos, Cleartrip, and OYO Rooms.

    Please visit the Ola Money section on your Ola app or download the Ola Money app to know more.
